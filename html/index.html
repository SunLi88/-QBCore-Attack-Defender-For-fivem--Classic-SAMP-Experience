How to Play

  • Attackers: Go to the marked objective zone and stay inside to capture it.
  • Defenders: Eliminate all attackers or prevent the capture before time runs out.
  • The team with the most round wins takes the match.
  • Leaving the zone cancels any ongoing capture.

Controls

  • [F5] — Open / close this menu
  • Objective zone is automatic — stay inside as an attacker

EXP Rewards

  • +50 EXP per kill
  • +100 EXP for winning a round
  • +20 EXP for losing a round
  • +50 EXP for a draw
